1. What is RepoRadar? The Big Idea

At its core, RepoRadar is an AI-powered platform built to help you analyze, discover, and compare GitHub repositories. It uses advanced AI, including Google's Gemini, to move beyond basic statistics and provide deep, qualitative insights into a project's health, quality, and potential.

The process is designed to be simple and fast, giving you a comprehensive report in just a few steps:

1. Paste a URL Start by pasting a GitHub repository URL into the search bar or use keywords to find a project.
2. AI Analysis RepoRadar instantly gets to work, fetching key repository signals—like issues, pull requests, and dependencies—and applying its AI scoring model.
3. Get a Clear Report Within seconds, you receive an easy-to-understand report complete with scores, trends, plain-English summaries, and concrete recommendations.

At the heart of every analysis are four key metrics designed to give you a clear, actionable signal on what matters most.

* Health: Measures the overall stability and maintenance quality.
* Velocity: Assesses the release/merge cadence and responsiveness.
* Risk: Gauges the bus factor, single-maintainer risk, and archival signals.
* Security: Rates the dependency freshness and known vulnerabilities.

This powerful combination of simplicity and depth makes RepoRadar a valuable tool for anyone who relies on open-source software.
